Recycled Bags

(142 reviews)

Product Description

Manufacturing & Materials: Our premium recycled bags are crafted from 100% post-consumer waste plastic bags that have been collected, cleaned, and transformed through an innovative upcycling process. These durable, multipurpose bags give new life to discarded plastic shopping bags, grocery bags, and packaging materials that would otherwise pollute our environment. Each bag undergoes a specialized manufacturing process that melts down and reforms waste plastic bags into strong, lightweight, and water-resistant material. The recycled plastic is woven into durable fabric that maintains flexibility while providing exceptional strength for daily use.

Features & Benefits: Perfect for shopping, travel, or everyday carrying needs, these bags feature reinforced stitching and comfortable handles designed to withstand heavy loads. Available in various sizes and colors, our recycled bags can be customized with eco-friendly printing using soy-based inks. The bags feature a protective coating that enhances durability while maintaining their eco-friendly credentials. These bags are designed to replace hundreds of single-use plastic bags throughout their lifetime, breaking the cycle of plastic waste and creating a truly circular economy solution.

Environmental Impact

Plastic Waste Reduction

Each bag is made from approximately 50-75 discarded plastic bags, preventing them from entering landfills or polluting oceans.

Carbon Footprint

Reduces CO2 emissions by 80% compared to manufacturing new plastic bags from virgin materials.

Resource Conservation

Uses 70% less energy and 60% less water in production versus creating new plastic bags

Circular Economy

Creates a closed-loop system where waste plastic bags become new reusable products

Lifetime Impact

Replaces up to 1,000 single-use plastic bags over its lifespan, breaking the cycle of plastic waste

Superior Durability

Woven recycled plastic fabric provides exceptional strength and tear resistance, outlasting typical reusable bags by 3-5x.

Flexible Storage

Can be folded or rolled into compact size when not in use, with some models including integrated storage pouches.

Multipurpose Design

Great for groceries, shopping, travel, beach trips, gym gear, and everyday carrying needs.

Easy Maintenance

Simply wipe clean with damp cloth or hose down for more thorough cleaning. Air dries quickly.

Customizable

Available in various colors and prints, with options for custom branding using eco-friendly inks.

Recycled Plastic Source

Collected primarily from retail take-back programs and municipal recycling centers. All materials undergo rigorous cleaning and quality control before processing.

Plastic Transformation Process

Plastic bags are shredded, melted, and extruded into thin threads that are then woven into durable fabric. Our proprietary process maintains material strength while allowing for design flexibility.

Protective Coating

A water-based polyurethane coating provides water resistance without compromising the recyclability of the final product. This coating is free from harmful phthalates and PVC.

Thread & Stitching

High-strength recycled polyester thread is used for all stitching, with reinforced stress points at handles and seams for maximum durability.

End-of-Life Options

When the bag eventually wears out, it can be returned to us through our take-back program to be recycled into new products, completing the circular cycle.
